Security services can be broadly categorized into two main types: physical security services and cybersecurity services.
Physical Security Services:

- Guard Services:Security guards are employed to protect properties, people, and assets. They perform tasks such as patrolling, monitoring surveillance systems, and controlling access to premises.

- Access Control:This involves using systems like ID cards, biometrics, and security personnel to regulate who can enter certain areas.
- Video Surveillance:Closed-circuit television (CCTV) systems are used to monitor and record activities in specific locations.

- Intrusion Detection and Alarm Systems:** These systems detect unauthorized access attempts and trigger alarms.

- Perimeter Protection:Methods like fences, barriers, and security lighting help secure the outer boundaries of a property.

- Security Consulting: Experts assess risks, design security plans, and provide recommendations to enhance security measures.

CyberSecurity Services:

- Network Security: computer networks from unauthorized access, attacks, and data breaches.

- Endpoint Security: Securing individual devices such as computers, smartphones, and tablets from malware and other threats.

- Firewalls and Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS):These technologies help monitor and filter network traffic to prevent unauthorized access.
-

Data Encryption: Ensuring that sensitive data is transformed into unreadable code to prevent unauthorized access.


- < Assessment and Penetration Testing:** Identifying weaknesses in systems and networks through simulated attacks.

- Incident Response: Developing strategies to detect, respond to, and recover from cybersecurity incidents.

- Security Awareness Training: Educating employees and users about cybersecurity best practices to prevent social engineering attacks.

Overall, security services are crucial for safeguarding physical assets, sensitive information, and digital systems from various threats, ranging from theft and vandalism to hacking and data breaches. The specific services needed depend on the nature of the assets being protected and the potential risks involved.
